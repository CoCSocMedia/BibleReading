Psalms 147

1 Hallelujah! / For it is good to sing psalms to our God; / For it is pleasant; praise is fitting.

2 Jehovah builds up Jerusalem; / He gathers the outcasts of Israel.

3 He heals the brokenhearted / And binds up their wounds.

4 He counts the number of the stars; / He calls them all by their names.

5 Great is our Lord, and mighty in power; / His understanding is infinite.

6 Jehovah uplifts the lowly; / He brings the wicked down to the ground.

7 Sing unto Jehovah with thanksgiving; / Sing psalms to our God with the lyre;

8 Who covers the heavens with clouds, / Who prepares rain for the earth, / Who causes grass to grow on the mountains,

9 Who gives to the beast its food, / And to the young ravens who cry.

10 He takes no delight in the strength of horses; / Nor pleasure in the legs of man.

11 Jehovah takes pleasure in those who fear Him, / In those who hope in His lovingkindness.

12 Laud Jehovah, O Jerusalem; / Praise your God, O Zion;

13 For He has strengthened the bars of your gates; / He has blessed your children within you.

14 He makes peace in your borders; / He satisfies you with the finest wheat.

15 He sends forth His command to the earth; / His word runs very swiftly.

16 He gives snow like wool; / He scatters frost like ashes;

17 He casts His hail like crumbs — / Who can stand before His cold?

18 He sends forth His word and melts them; / He causes His wind to blow; the waters flow.

19 He declares His word to Jacob, / His statutes and His ordinances to Israel.

20 He has not dealt so with any other nation; / And as for His ordinances, they have not known them. / Hallelujah!
