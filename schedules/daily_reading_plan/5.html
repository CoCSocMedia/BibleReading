Psalms 114

1 When Israel went forth from Egypt, / The house of Jacob from among a people of unintelligible speech,

2 Judah became His sanctuary, / Israel, His dominion.

3 The sea saw and fled; / The Jordan turned back.

4 The mountains skipped like rams, / The hills, like lambs.

5 What troubles you, O sea, that you flee? / O Jordan, that you turn back?

6 O mountains, that you skip like rams? / O hills, like lambs?

7 Tremble at the presence of the Lord, O earth, / At the presence of the God of Jacob,

8 Who turns the rock into a pool of water, / The flint into a fountain of water.
