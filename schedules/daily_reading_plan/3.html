Psalms 112

1 Hallelujah! / Blessed is the man who fears Jehovah, / Who delights greatly in His commandments.

2 His seed will be mighty on the earth; / The generation of the upright will be blessed.

3 Wealth and riches are in his house, / And his righteousness endures forever.

4 Light in the darkness arises to the upright; / They are gracious and compassionate and righteous.

5 It is well with the man who is gracious and lends; / He will maintain his cause in judgment.

6 For he will never be moved; / The righteous man will be remembered forever.

7 He will not be afraid of evil tidings; / His heart is steadfast, trusting in Jehovah.

8 His heart is sustained, he is not afraid, / Until he sees what should happen to his adversaries.

9 He has scattered abroad; he has given to the needy; / His righteousness endures forever; / His horn will be exalted with honor.

10 The wicked man will see it and be angry; / He will gnash his teeth and melt away; / The desire of the wicked will perish.
