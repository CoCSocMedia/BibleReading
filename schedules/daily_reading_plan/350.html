Psalms 94

1 O God of vengeance, O Jehovah, / O God of vengeance, shine forth!

2 Lift Yourself up, O Judge of the earth; / Render the reward upon the proud.

3 How long, O Jehovah, will the wicked, / How long will the wicked exult?

4 They burst out; they speak arrogantly; / All the workers of iniquity boast.

5 They crush Your people, O Jehovah, / And afflict Your inheritance.

6 They slay the widow and the stranger, / And they murder orphans.

7 And they say, Jehovah does not see, / And the God of Jacob does not consider.

8 Consider, you senseless ones among the people; / And you foolish ones, when will you become wise?

9 He who planted the ear, does He not hear? / And He who formed the eye, does He not see?

10 He who disciplines the nations, / He who teaches man knowledge, does He not reprove?

11 Jehovah knows the thoughts of man, / That they are vanity.

12 Blessed is the man / Whom You discipline, O Jehovah, / And teach out of Your law;

13 That You may give him rest from the days of evil / Until the pit is dug for the wicked.

14 For Jehovah will not abandon His people, / Nor will He forsake His inheritance.

15 For judgment will return unto righteousness, / And all the upright in heart will follow it.

16 Who will rise up for me against the evildoers? / Who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?

17 Unless Jehovah had been my help, / My soul would have soon dwelt in silence.

18 When I said, My foot has slipped, / Your lovingkindness, O Jehovah, upheld me.

19 When my disquieting thoughts within me are many, / Your comfortings delight my soul.

20 Shall the seat of destruction be united with You, / Which devises trouble through its statutes?

21 They band together against the soul of the righteous man / And condemn innocent blood.

22 But Jehovah has been my high tower, / And my God the rock of my refuge;

23 And He will return their iniquity upon them / And destroy them in their own evil; / Jehovah our God will destroy them.
