Psalms 113

1 Hallelujah! / Praise, you servants of Jehovah, / Praise the name of Jehovah.

2 Let the name of Jehovah be blessed, / From now and to eternity.

3 From the rising of the sun to its setting / The name of Jehovah is to be praised.

4 Jehovah is high above all the nations, / And His glory is above the heavens.

5 Who is like Jehovah our God, / Who has set His throne on high,

6 Who humbles Himself to behold / The heavens and the earth?

7 He raises the poor from the dust; / From the ash heap He lifts the needy;

8 That He may seat them with princes, / With the princes of His people.

9 He causes the barren woman to keep house / As a joyful mother of children. / Hallelujah.
