Psalms 148

1 Hallelujah! / Praise Jehovah from the heavens; / Praise Him in the heights.

2 Praise Him, all His angels; / Praise Him, all His hosts.

3 Praise Him, sun and moon; / Praise Him, all stars of light.

4 Praise Him, you heaven of heavens, / And you waters that are above the heavens.

5 Let them praise the name of Jehovah; / For He commanded, and they were created.

6 And He established them forever and ever; / He gave the decree which shall not pass away.

7 Praise Jehovah from the earth, / You sea monsters and all the depths;

8 Fire and hail, snow and smoke; / Stormy wind that executes His word;

9 Mountains and all hills; / Fruit trees and all cedars;

10 All animals wild and tame; / Creeping things and birds of flight;

11 Kings of the earth and all peoples; / Princes and all judges of the earth;

12 Choice young men as well as virgins; / Old men with young men.

13 Let them praise the name of Jehovah, / For His name alone is exalted; / His glory is above the earth and the heavens.

14 And He has raised up a horn for His people, / The praise of all His faithful ones, / The children of Israel, a people near to Him. / Hallelujah!
