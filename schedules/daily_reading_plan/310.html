Psalms 54

1 O God, save me by Your name, / And execute judgment for me by Your might.

2 O God, hear my prayer; / Give ear to the words of my mouth.

3 For strangers have risen up against me, / And those who terrorize seek my life; / They do not set God before them. Selah

4 Behold, God is my Helper; / The Lord is among those who sustain my soul.

5 He will return the evil to those who lie in wait for me. / Annihilate them in Your faithfulness.

6 I will offer a freewill offering to You; / I will praise Your name, O Jehovah, for it is good.

7 For He has delivered me from all distress, / And my eye looks triumphantly upon my enemies.
