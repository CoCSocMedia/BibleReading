Psalms 134

1 Bless Jehovah now, / All you servants of Jehovah / Who stand by night in the house of Jehovah.

2 Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, / And bless Jehovah.

3 May Jehovah, who made heaven and earth, / Bless you from Zion.
