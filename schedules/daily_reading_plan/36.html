Psalms 145

1 I will extol You, O my God and King; / And I will bless Your name forever and ever.

2 Every day I will bless You, / And I will praise Your name forever and ever.

3 Great is Jehovah, and much to be praised; / And His greatness is unsearchable.

4 One generation will laud Your works to another, / And they will declare Your mighty acts.

5 Upon the glorious splendor of Your majesty / And upon Your wondrous works I will muse.

6 And men will speak of the might of Your awesome deeds, / And I will declare Your greatness.

7 They will utter the memory of Your great goodness / And will sing for joy of Your righteousness.

8 Jehovah is gracious and compassionate, / Long-suffering and great in lovingkindness.

9 Jehovah is good to all, / And His compassions are upon all His works.

10 All Your works will praise You, O Jehovah; / And Your faithful ones will bless You.

11 They will speak of the glory of Your kingdom / And tell of Your power,

12 To make known to the sons of men Your mighty acts / And the glorious splendor of Your kingdom.

13 Your kingdom is an eternal kingdom, / And Your dominion is throughout all generations.

14 Jehovah upholds all who are falling / And raises up all who are bowed down.

15 The eyes of everyone wait for You, / And You give them their food in due season.

16 You open up Your hand / And satisfy the desire of every living thing.

17 Jehovah is righteous in all His ways, / And faithfully kind in all His doings.

18 Jehovah is near to all who call upon Him, / To all who call upon Him in truth.

19 He will fulfill the desire of those who fear Him, / And He will hear their cry and save them.

20 Jehovah preserves all who love Him, / But He will destroy all the wicked.

21 My mouth will speak the praise of Jehovah, / And all flesh will bless His holy name forever and ever.
