Psalms 66

1 Shout joyfully to God, all the earth;

2 Sing forth the glory of His name; / Make glorious His praise.

3 Say to God, How awesome are Your deeds! / Because of the greatness of Your strength, Your enemies come cringing to You.

4 All the earth will worship You / And sing psalms to You; / They will sing psalms to Your name. Selah

5 Come and see the works of God; / He is awesome in His deeds toward the sons of men.

6 He turned the sea into dry land; / Through the river they crossed on foot. / There we rejoiced in Him.

7 He rules by His might forever; / His eyes observe the nations: / Let not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah

8 Bless our God, O peoples; / And make the voice of His praise heard,

9 Who has kept our soul in life / And does not allow our feet to slip.

10 For You have tried us, O God; / You have refined us as silver is refined.

11 You have brought us into the net; / You have put a heavy burden on our loins.

12 You have made men ride over our heads; / We have gone through fire and water; / But You have brought us forth into abundance.

13 I will come into Your house with burnt offerings; / I will pay my vows to You,

14 Which my lips uttered / And my mouth spoke when I was in distress.

15 I will offer to You burnt offerings of fatlings / With the smoke of rams; / I will offer cattle with goats. Selah

16 Come, hear, all you who fear God, / And I will recount what He has done for my soul.

17 To Him I called out with my mouth, / And He was extolled with my tongue.

18 If I regarded iniquity in my heart, / The Lord did not hear.

19 But indeed God has heard; / He has given heed to the voice of my prayer.

20 Blessed be God, / Who has not turned my prayer away / Nor His lovingkindness from me.
