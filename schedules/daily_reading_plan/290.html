Psalms 34

1 I will bless Jehovah at all times; / His praise will continually be in my mouth.

2 My soul makes its boast in Jehovah; / The lowly hear and they rejoice.

3 Magnify Jehovah with me, / And let us exalt His name together.

4 I sought Jehovah, and He answered me; / And He delivered me from all that terrified me.

5 They looked to Him and were radiant; / And their faces will never be abashed.

6 This poor man called out, and Jehovah heard; / And He saved him out of all his troubles.

7 The angel of Jehovah encamps / Around those who fear Him, and He delivers them.

8 Taste and see that Jehovah is good; / Blessed is the man who takes refuge in Him.

9 Fear Jehovah, you who are His saints; / For there is no lack with those who fear Him.

10 The young lions hunger and starve, / But those who seek Jehovah will not lack any good thing.

11 Come, children; hear me. / I will teach you the fear of Jehovah.

12 Who is the man who desires life, / Who loves having days in order to see good?

13 Guard your tongue from evil, / And your lips from speaking deceit.

14 Turn away from evil and do good; / Seek peace and pursue it.

15 The eyes of Jehovah are set toward the righteous, / And His ears, toward their cry.

16 The face of Jehovah is against those who do evil, / To cut off the memory of them from the earth.

17 The righteous cry out, and Jehovah hears; / And He saves them out of all their troubles.

18 Jehovah is near to those who are broken in heart, / And He saves those who are contrite in spirit.

19 Many are the troubles of the righteous man, / But Jehovah delivers him from all of them.

20 He keeps all his bones; / Not one of them is broken.

21 Evil will slay the wicked, / And those who hate the righteous man will be condemned.

22 Jehovah redeems the soul of His servants, / And none who take refuge in Him will be condemned.
