Psalms 87

1 His foundation is in the holy mountains.

2 Jehovah loves the gates of Zion / More than all the dwellings of Jacob.

3 Glorious things are spoken of you, / O city of God. Selah

4 I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon as those who know Me; / Behold, Philistia and Tyre along with Cush: / This one was born there, they say.

5 But of Zion it will be said, / This one and that one were born in her, / And the Most High Himself will establish her.

6 Jehovah will count / When He records the peoples: / This One was born there. Selah

7 Then singing as well as dancing, they will say, / All my springs are in you.
