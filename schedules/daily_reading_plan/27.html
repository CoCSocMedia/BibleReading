Psalms 136

1 Give thanks to Jehovah, for He is good; / For His lovingkindness is forever.

2 Give thanks to the God of gods, / For His lovingkindness is forever.

3 Give thanks to the Lord of lords, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

4 To Him who alone does great wonders, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

5 To Him who by understanding made the heavens, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

6 To Him who spread forth the earth over the waters, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

7 To Him who made the great lights, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

8 The sun for ruling the day, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

9 The moon and the stars for ruling the night, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

10 To Him who struck Egypt in their firstborn, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

11 And brought forth Israel from their midst, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

12 With a mighty hand and an outstretched arm, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

13 To Him who divided the Red Sea apart, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

14 And brought Israel over through the midst of it, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

15 And shook off Pharaoh and his force into the Red Sea, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

16 To Him who marched His people through the wilderness, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

17 To Him who struck great kings, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

18 And slew majestic kings, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

19 Sihon the king of the Amorites, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

20 And Og the king of Bashan, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

21 And gave their land as an inheritance, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

22 An inheritance to Israel His servant, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

23 Who remembered us in our low estate, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

24 And rescued us from our adversaries, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

25 Who gives food to all flesh, / For His lovingkindness is forever.

26 Give thanks to the God of heaven, / For His lovingkindness is forever.
