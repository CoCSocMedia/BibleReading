Psalms 33

1 Rejoice in Jehovah, O righteous ones. / Praise is becoming to the upright.

2 Give thanks to Jehovah with the lyre; / With the harp of ten strings sing psalms to Him.

3 Sing Him a new song; / Play skillfully with a joyous shout.

4 For the word of Jehovah is right, / And all His work is done in faithfulness.

5 He loves righteousness and justice; / The earth is full of the lovingkindness of Jehovah.

6 By the word of Jehovah the heavens were made, / And all their host, by the breath of His mouth.

7 He gathers the water of the sea like a heap; / He puts the deep waters into storage places.

8 Let all the earth fear Jehovah; / Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of Him.

9 For He spoke, and it was; / He commanded, and it stood.

10 Jehovah brings the counsel of the nations to nothing; / He frustrates the intentions of the peoples.

11 The counsel of Jehovah stands forever; / The intentions of His heart stand from generation to generation.

12 Blessed is the nation whose God is Jehovah, / The people whom He has chosen as His own possession.

13 Jehovah looks from heaven; / He sees all the sons of men.

14 From the site of His habitation He gazes / At all the inhabitants of the earth,

15 He who fashions the hearts of them all, / He who discerns all their works.

16 No king is saved by a great army; / A mighty man is not delivered by great strength.

17 A horse is a vain thing for salvation, / And it cannot deliver by its great power.

18 Behold, the eye of Jehovah is upon those who fear Him, / Upon those who hope in His lovingkindness,

19 To deliver their soul from death / And to preserve them alive in famine.

20 Our soul waits on Jehovah; / He is our help and our shield;

21 For in Him our heart rejoices, / Because in His holy name we trust.

22 May Your lovingkindness, O Jehovah, be upon us, / Even as we have hoped in You.
