Psalms 48

1 Great is Jehovah, / And much to be praised / In the city of our God, / In His holy mountain.

2 Beautiful in elevation, / The joy of the whole earth, / Is Mount Zion, the sides of the north, / The city of the great King.

3 God has made Himself known / In her palaces as a high retreat.

4 For, behold, the kings have assembled themselves; / They passed by together.

5 They saw; therefore they were astounded. / They were dismayed; they hurried off in alarm.

6 Trembling seized them there; / Writhing, like that of a woman in labor;

7 With an east wind You shattered / The ships of Tarshish.

8 As we have heard, / So we have seen / In the city of Jehovah of hosts, / In the city of our God. / God will establish her forever. Selah

9 We consider, O God, Your lovingkindness / In the midst of Your temple.

10 As Your name is, O God, / So is Your praise / Unto the ends of the earth; / Your right hand is full of righteousness.

11 Let Mount Zion rejoice; / Let the daughters of Judah exult / Because of Your judgments.

12 Walk about Zion, and go around her; / Count her towers.

13 Consider carefully her bulwarks; / Pass between her palaces; / That you may recount it to the generation following.

14 For this God is our God forever and ever. / He will guide us even unto death.
